About Monica

Monica Chew is a Bay Area pianist. I founded Minsky Duo with Nato, and Duo Moderna with Jerry Kuderna. I prefer chamber music to concerti, Haydn to Mozart, and lately Bartok above all things. My favorite thing is to produce concerts with a strong narrative arc.

Previously, I worked as a software engineer at Mozilla and Google. If you are looking for my technology vita, please see my Linkedin page.
